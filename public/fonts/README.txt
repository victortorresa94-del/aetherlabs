ADVERCASE FONT — Place files here
==================================
Download the free personal version from:
https://indieground.net/product/advercase-font/

Required files:
- Advercase.woff2  (primary)
- Advercase.woff   (fallback)
- Advercase-Bold.woff2 (optional bold weight)

Once placed here, the font will auto-activate in the hero section.
The @font-face is already configured in src/app/globals.css.
